📈 Instagram Engagement Rate Standards

A good Instagram engagement rate is typically above 1%, but this varies significantly based on follower count. Nano-influencers (1K-10K followers) and micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) consistently achieve the highest engagement rates, often exceeding 3-6%.

Our engagement rate calculator helps you identify these high-performing creators. Mega-influencers and celebrities (500K+ followers) typically see lower rates (1-3%) due to their massive audience size.

How to Calculate Instagram Engagement Rate?

Our Instagram engagement rate calculator uses the most accurate method: we calculate the median number of likes and comments per post, then divide by the total follower count.

This engagement rate formula gives you a realistic picture of how engaged an Instagram audience truly is, filtering out viral posts that might skew the average.

💡 Pro Tip: A good Instagram engagement rate is typically above 1%, with 3%+ being excellent for most account sizes.

Instagram Engagement Rate Calculator FAQ

Everything you need to know about calculating Instagram engagement rates

How to check influencer engagement rate?

Type any public Instagram profile into our Instagram engagement rate calculator to get their up-to-date engagement rate. If you're unsure where to start, use our influencer search platform to find influencers.

What affects engagement rate?

The two main factors that affect engagement rate are the number of followers and engagement–likes, comments, shares, etc.- that a post receives. The total engagement, often summarized as likes, divided by the follower count, is the engagement rate. Typically, nano- and micro-influencers have the highest engagement rates.

Things that affect the engagement rate of a specific post are often related to how on-brand that piece of content is. Does it meet the audience's expectations for the kind of content that influencer produces? To avoid making sponsored posts feel inauthentic, use an influencer gifting platform like Social Cat to find top influencers by niche.

How does engagement rate impact ROI?

Engagement rates measure trust and interest. The more invested an audience is in an influencer's content, the more likely they are to buy something the influencer recommends. So, a higher engagement rate generally means higher ROI, too.

How to use the engagement calculator?

Type the name of the profile whose Instagram engagement rate you'd like to check–the @ symbol followed by their handle. Keep all punctuation and spelling the same. Also, make sure their profile is public–we can't calculate the engagement rate for private Instagram accounts!

Why check an influencer's engagement rate?

To know how much trust people place in an influencer, look at their engagement rate. Engagement tracks the number of people who saw the influencer's post and cared about it enough to interact with it. If an influencer has a low engagement rate, they don't know how to appeal to their core audience–and that usually means your collaborations won't perform well, either.

How do you calculate the engagement rate per post on Instagram?

Our Instagram engagement rate calculator tells you an influencer's overall engagement rate. If you'd like to calculate the engagement rate for a specific post, do the following: Add up the number of likes, comments, and shares on the post, then divide by their follower count–preferably, their follower count when the post was created. This will give you an accurate picture of how much their audience cares about that content.

What is a high engagement rate on Instagram?

This varies according to an Instagram influencer's number of followers and niche–see the charts further up this page for more information. However, a high Instagram engagement rate generally starts at 2.5%.

How can you see the engagement rate on Instagram?

Instagram doesn't broadcast post engagement rates. If you want to see engagement rates on Instagram, use an engagement rate calculator like the one on this page.

How to improve Instagram engagement rate?

To improve your engagement rate, try the following:

  • Ask for engagement–end your videos with a question to your audience for the comment section and request that they like, save, or share your content if they loved it.
  • Interact with your followers. Respond to comments in the comments section or with an entire video. This can be a great way to fuel engagement–just make sure you're shouting out the kind of responses you like to see.
  • Don't be overly salesy with promotions. Our influencer marketing report suggests that integrating promotional products naturally into your content is the most effective way to preserve your engagement rate on sponsored posts.
